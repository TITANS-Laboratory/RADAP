Our Solution

Three Integrated Technology Pillars

RADAP combines AI, blockchain, and community training to transform how farmers report and recover from agricultural disasters.

ü§ñ

AI-Driven Dynamic Questionnaires

Questionnaires that automatically adapt based on weather event type, storm parameters, and farm operation profile. Winter storms surface structural and livestock questions; tornadoes prioritize debris and equipment; floods focus on contamination and crop submersion.

‚õìÔ∏è

Blockchain-Secured Data

Every damage report is cryptographically hashed and stored on an immutable distributed ledger. Smart contracts enforce privacy ‚Äî only the farmer and their authorized agents can access raw data. Aggregated, anonymized analytics serve regional responders.

üö®

AI Emergency Alerts

A central analytics engine processes incoming reports in near-real time using NLP and computer vision. When critical thresholds are detected ‚Äî structural collapse clusters, widespread livestock loss ‚Äî automated alerts reach emergency responders with severity rankings and coordinates.

Assessment Framework

Eight Damage Categories for Winter Storms

Developed from USDA disaster assistance categories and verified Storm Fern agricultural impact reports.

01

Structural Damage

Barn collapses, roof failures, fencing breakdowns from snow and ice weight

02

Livestock Losses

Mortality from cold exposure, frozen water supplies, inability to maintain shelter

03

Equipment Damage

Mechanical failures, frozen pipes and pumps, generator overuse or failure

04

Feed & Forage

Damaged stored hay/feed, lost grazing materials, inaccessible feed stocks

05

Crop Damage

Winter wheat root damage from freeze-thaw, cover crop losses, delayed fieldwork

06

Power & Technology

Precision ag sensor failures, connectivity losses, cold storage failures

07

Transportation

Impassable roads, delayed commodity movement, blocked farm access

08

Food Safety

Compromised cold storage from power outages, spoiled perishables, contamination

Program Alignment

Addressing All Three A1712 Emphasis Areas

Emphasis Area 1

Agroecosystem Resilience

Rapid, comprehensive damage documentation accelerates recovery timelines and generates regional datasets that inform future preparedness strategies.

Emphasis Area 2

Food Safety, Nutrition Security & Commodity Security

Identifies cold storage failures, contamination risks, and supply chain disruptions to support rapid interventions protecting the food supply.

Emphasis Area 3

Health, Well-Being & Safety

Reduces administrative burden during acute stress, connects producers to assistance faster, and flags hazardous conditions like structural instabilities and CO risks.
